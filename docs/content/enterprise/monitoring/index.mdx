Enterprise monitoring is about knowing whether connected environments are healthy, reachable, and operating inside the expected organization context.

**Audience:** Enterprise operators and administrators

## What To Watch

At a high level, monitor:

- connected workspace or runtime health
- enterprise connection state
- access and sign-in issues that block operators
- authority activity trends that need review
- workflow or reporting jobs that are delayed or failing

## Healthy Operating Signals

A healthy enterprise-connected environment usually shows:

- successful sign-in for authorized users
- expected workspace visibility
- normal activity and recent status updates
- no unexplained disconnects or access failures

## Common Monitoring Workflows

### Daily review

Perform a quick review of:

- current environment status
- recent operational activity
- user-reported access problems

### Change review

After access, configuration, or rollout changes, confirm that:

- the environment remains connected
- the correct teams still have access
- expected workflows and reporting surfaces still function

### Escalation review

Escalate when you see:

- repeated connection failures
- persistent sign-in problems
- unexpected drops in visibility or activity
- reporting or workflow operations that do not complete
