# Caracal Enterprise Edition

Caracal Enterprise Edition is a comprehensive platform designed for organizations that require robust security, compliance, and advanced management capabilities for their agentic workflows. It extends the power of Caracal Core and Flow with features tailored for enterprise-scale deployments.

## Why Enterprise?

### Security and Compliance
- **Fail-Closed Security**: By default, all operations are denied unless explicitly allowed by policy.
- **Audit Logs**: Detailed logs of all agent activities and policy enforcements for compliance and auditing.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Granular control over who can manage agents, policies, and configurations.

### Advanced Management
- **Centralized Dashboard**: A unified view of all your agents, policies, and their status.
- **Workspace Isolation**: Securely isolate different teams or projects within the same instance.
- **Enterprise Sync**: Seamlessly synchronize policies and configurations across multiple Caracal Core instances.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Tenancy**: Support for multiple organizations and workspaces with strict data isolation.
- **SSO Integration**: Integrate with your existing identity providers (IdP) for seamless authentication.
- **Premium Support**: Access to dedicated support channels and priority issue resolution.
