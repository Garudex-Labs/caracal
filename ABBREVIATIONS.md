# ABBREVIATIONS

All environment variable prefixes and shorthand tokens used in this repository.

---

## Prefix Legend

| Prefix | Stands for |
|--------|-----------|
| `CCL_` | Caracal (open-source core) |
| `CCLE_` | Caracal Enterprise |
